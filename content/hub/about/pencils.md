Pencil work in Lumi is meant for construction, exploration, and early decision-making. Loose sketching, planning shapes, and refining forms all need tools that feel immediate and controlled without becoming heavy or overcomplicated.

This part of the workflow supports building drawings in stages, from rough gesture to more deliberate structure, while keeping the process fast enough to stay close to hand-drawn thinking.
