Lumi’s workspace is designed for focused painting rather than general-purpose image editing. Panels, tools, and views are arranged to keep the drawing process clear, responsive, and easy to manage during long sessions.

The aim is structural clarity: layers stay readable, colour tools stay close at hand, and the interface reduces distractions that do not support illustration work. As the software grows, the workspace continues to be shaped around real production use rather than feature sprawl.
