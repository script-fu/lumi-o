Inking in Lumi is aimed at deliberate line work: controlled strokes, clear edge definition, and a workflow that supports finishing rather than fighting the tool. Pressure, spacing, and brush behaviour are tuned toward consistency and repeatability.

The goal is to make clean line work feel dependable, whether it is being used for illustration, comics-style rendering, or tightly structured painted edges.
